24-Hour Data Lifecycle

All files uploaded to your DATEXIA session — images, documents, and sync streams — carry a strict 24-hour Time-To-Live (TTL) metadata marker.

After 24 hours, an automated background process permanently and irreversibly deletes all session assets from cloud servers. No backups. No logs. Your customers' documents are never retained.

⚠ Download your files before the session expires. Once deleted, recovery is impossible.
